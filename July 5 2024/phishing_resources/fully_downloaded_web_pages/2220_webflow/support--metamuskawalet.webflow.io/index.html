Launched in 2016, MetaMask rose to popularity soon after its establishment and there are many known aspects that lead to the same. If you are checking out this read for getting a brief introduction about this wallet and how you can use the MetaMask wallet extension, then you have landed on the right page. First of all, I would like you to know that MetaMask is a blockchain technology company which is known as ConsenSys. The wallet lets you:

  • Connect your web browser to the Ethereum blockchain
  • This in turn lets you buy, sell, and trade Ethereum
  • In addition to that, it also allows you to work with ERC-20 tokens and NFTs
  • Once you download MetaMask, you can also access decentralized applications (dApps) easily
  • Users can also access decentralized finance assets as well as decentralized exchanges.
