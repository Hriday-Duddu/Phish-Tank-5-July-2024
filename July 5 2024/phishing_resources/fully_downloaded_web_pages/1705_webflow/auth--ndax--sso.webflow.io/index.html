How to Log In to Your NDAX Account

Logging into your NDAX account is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to access your account securely:

  1. Visit the NDAX Website: Open your preferred web browser and go to the official NDAX website at www.ndax.io.
  2. Click on the 'Login' Button: On the homepage, locate the 'Login' button, typically found in the top right corner of the screen. Click on it to proceed to the login page.
  3. Enter Your Credentials:
    • Email Address: Enter the email address associated with your NDAX account.
    • Password: Input your account password. Ensure your password is strong and unique to maintain account security.
  4. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):
    • If you have enabled 2FA, you will be prompted to enter the authentication code sent to your registered mobile device or generated by your authentication app (such as Google Authenticator).
    • Enter the 2FA code to proceed.
  5. Access Your Account: After entering your credentials and the 2FA code (if applicable), click on the 'Login' button. You will be redirected to your account dashboard, where you can manage your portfolio, execute trades, and access other features.
