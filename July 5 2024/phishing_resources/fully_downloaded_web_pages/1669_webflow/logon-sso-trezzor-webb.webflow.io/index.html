How to Log In to Trezor

Logging in to your Trezor device is straightforward:

  1. Connect Your Device: Use the provided USB cable to connect your Trezor to your computer.
  2. Open Trezor Suite: Launch the Trezor Suite application on your computer.
  3. Enter PIN: On your Trezor device, enter your PIN. The device will show a scrambled number pad; use this to enter your PIN.
  4. Confirm Login: After entering your PIN, confirm any additional security prompts that appear in Trezor Suite.
  5. Access Your Wallet: Once logged in, you can access your wallets, manage accounts, and perform transactions securely.

For detailed instructions, visit the Trezor website.
