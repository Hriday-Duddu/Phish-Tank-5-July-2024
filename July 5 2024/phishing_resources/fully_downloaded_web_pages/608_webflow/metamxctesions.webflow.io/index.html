MetaMask® Extension - Browser extension - webflow

MetaMask is a browser extension that enables users to interact with Ethereum-based DApps directly from their web browser. It acts as a wallet, allowing you to manage your Ethereum assets and seamlessly engage with decentralized applications. Here's how you can use MetaMask extension:

  1. Install MetaMask Extension:Start by installing the MetaMask extension for your preferred web browser (such as Google Chrome or Mozilla Firefox). Go to the official MetaMask website or your browser's extension store and add the extension.
  2. Create or Import a Wallet:Once the extension is installed, click on the MetaMask icon in your browser's toolbar. You'll be prompted to create a new wallet or import an existing one using a recovery seed phrase.
  3. Access a DApp:With MetaMask installed and your wallet set up, you can now navigate to Ethereum-based decentralized applications (DApps) that support MetaMask. When you encounter a DApp, you'll typically see a "Connect" or "Connect Wallet" button. Click on it.
  4. Authorize Connection:A MetaMask pop-up will appear, asking you to authorize the DApp to access your wallet. Confirm the connection, and the DApp will be granted access to interact with your wallet for that session.
  5. Interact with the DApp:After connecting your wallet, you can now use the DApp's features. This might include actions like trading, borrowing, lending, gaming, and more, depending on the DApp's functionality.
  6. Confirm Transactions:Some actions in the DApp, such as making trades or interacting with smart contracts, might require transaction confirmations. MetaMask will display a pop-up with transaction details. Review and confirm the transaction to proceed.
  7. Monitor and Manage:Use MetaMask to monitor your wallet balance, transaction history, and interact with other DApps within the Ethereum ecosystem.
